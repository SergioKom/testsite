  • Photo London

    16 - 19 May 2019
    Dellasposa’s unprecedented involvement in Photo London will manifest in a solo show dedicated to Husak, a London-based, contemporary and process-driven artist. In 2018, Husak graduated with a Masters from the Royal College of Art, having specialised in the expanded field of photography and print. Through Dellasposa’s representation, Husak will showcase artworks from the ‘Mirrored’ and ‘Trace’ series. Husak’s photographs, comprised of pigment transfer and carbon pencil, are impressed upon sandblasted aluminium.
